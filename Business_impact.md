## Business Insights

- Enabled identification of high-risk customers based on credit utilization patterns, supporting proactive credit risk management and reducing potential defaults.

- Helped recognize high-value and long-tenure customers, allowing the bank to design loyalty programs and offer premium financial services.

- Revealed customers with high credit limits but low balances, creating opportunities for targeted upselling of loans, credit products, and investment services.

- Customer segmentation by age, income, and marital status supports personalized marketing strategies and product recommendations.

- Financial behavior analysis assists in improving customer retention, detecting dormant accounts, and strengthening overall portfolio quality.
